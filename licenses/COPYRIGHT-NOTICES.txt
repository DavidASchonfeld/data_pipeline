================================================================================
COPYRIGHT NOTICES
================================================================================

This file records the verbatim upstream copyright line for each third-party
component listed in ../THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md, grouped by license family.

Why this file exists: the MIT and BSD licenses embed a per-project copyright
holder in their text. Rather than duplicate the (identical) license body once
per dependency, this project keeps ONE canonical body per family (MIT.txt,
BSD-3-Clause.txt, Apache-2.0.txt, ...) and pairs it with the specific copyright
line below. Canonical body + the matching line here = the retained "copyright
notice and license text" each permissive license requires.

Copyright lines were copied from each project's upstream LICENSE/NOTICE file.
Lines marked (best-effort) could not be read from a machine-readable LICENSE and
are taken from the project's documented authorship — verify before relying on
them commercially. This inventory is best-effort and is not legal advice.
